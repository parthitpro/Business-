Customer Discovery Playbook

Ask.Don't Pitch.

Talk to 10 local businesses. Listen more than you speak. The problem that shows up 7 out of 10 times — that's what you build.

PHASE 01 Warm Up Build trust first
01How long have you been running this business?
02How many people work with you?
03Walk me through a typical day — what does your morning look like?
04What part of the business do YOU personally handle every day?
PHASE 02 Find The Pain Find what hurts
05What's the most frustrating part of running this business?
06What keeps you up at night about the business?
07What do you do today that you wish someone else could handle?
08Where do you feel you're losing money but don't know how to fix it?
09If I gave you one extra hour every day, what would you do with it?
10What's something that breaks down or goes wrong repeatedly?
PHASE 03 Leads & Customers Find revenue leaks
11How do new customers find you?
12How many inquiries do you get per day — calls, WhatsApp, walk-ins?
13What happens when you miss a call or can't reply immediately?
14How do you follow up with someone who showed interest but didn't buy?
15How many leads do you think you lose every month?
16Do customers come back on their own or do you have to remind them?
17How do you stay in touch with old customers?
PHASE 04 Marketing & Visibility Spot content gap
18Are you on Instagram, Facebook, Google?
19Who manages your social media right now?
20How often do you post content?
21Do you run any ads? How do you decide what to spend?
22How do customers find out about your offers or new products?
23Do you ask customers for Google reviews? What happens?
PHASE 05 Operations & Staff Find manual work
24How do you track orders, payments, and deliveries?
25Do you use any software or is it all WhatsApp and Excel?
26How do you know if your staff is doing their job?
27What happens when you're not there — does the business run smoothly?
28How do you handle stock — do you ever run out or overstock?
29How long does it take to train a new employee?
PHASE 06 Money & Numbers Build trust first
30Do you have a clear picture of your profit every month?
31How do you track expenses — any system or rough estimate?
32Are there months where business is very slow? How do you manage?
33Do you give credit to customers? How do you track who owes what?
34Have you ever lost money because of a mistake you could have avoided?
PHASE 07 Tech Awareness Test readiness
35Do you use any apps or tools to run the business?
36Have you ever tried automating anything — even simple things?
37Have you heard of businesses using AI or WhatsApp bots?
38If something could save you 2 hours every day, what would that be worth to you?
39What would make you say yes to a new tool — what's your biggest concern?
PHASE 08 — MOST IMPORTANT

The Dream Question

"If your business was running perfectly 2 years from now — what would that look like? What would YOU be doing every day?"
→ Always end with this. Their answer tells you exactly what to build.

How To Run The Conversation

⏱
Time needed20–30 minutes per business
🎯
Your attitudeCurious student, not salesman
🚫
Never say"I have a solution for this"
✅
Always say"Interesting, tell me more about that"
📝
Take notesOn phone, openly — they feel heard
🔚
End with"Can I come back and share something I'm working on?"
🚩 Red Flags — Move On
❌ "We're managing fine, no problems"
❌ "I don't trust technology"
❌ "My son/nephew handles all this"
❌ "We've always done it this way"
✅ Green Flags — This Is Your Customer
✅ "I'm always so busy I can't think straight"
✅ "I lose so many leads, I know it"
✅ "My WhatsApp is flooded and I can't reply"
✅ "I wish I didn't have to be here every day"
✅ "I spend hours on things that feel pointless"
